For defence and space investors this test validates the heavy lift architecture underpinning future NASA Artemis missions and US military launch contracts.

🛡️ Defence
BREAKING USA
Pentagon awards USD 500M counter-drone contract to startup
The Department of Defense has awarded Perennial Autonomy a USD 500 million contract to accelerate procurement of counter-drone defensive systems. The California-based startup has emerged as a key player in the rapidly growing counter-UAS market. This contract signals sustained Pentagon investment in autonomous threat defeat capabilities as drone warfare intensifies globally.
NORMAL USA
Pentagon deploys AI targeting to defeat drone threats
The Department of Defense is integrating AI-enhanced target recognition systems that can distinguish drones from non-threats such as birds faster than any human operator. The C-UAS Close-In Kinetic Defeat Enhancement project covers troops vehicles and ships. This represents a significant shift toward AI-enabled autonomous defence and will drive procurement demand for AI-integrated platforms.
NORMAL INDIA
India pushes for Rafale tech transfer amid sovereignty drive
India is facing resistance from France over Integrated Configuration Data access for Rafale jets as it pursues domestic defence production sovereignty. Supplier states are reluctant to transfer sensitive technical data even under formal agreements. This underscores the strategic tension between arms sales and technology independence relevant to all Middle East and Asian defence buyers.
