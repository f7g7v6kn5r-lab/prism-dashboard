BREAKING North America CRITICAL · 90
Pentagon Seeks MQ-9 Reaper Replacements via DIU
The Defense Innovation Unit issued a notice on Jul 8 2026 seeking a Massed Modular Aircraft (MMA) -- a cheaper, mass-producible drone capable of performing MQ-9A missions -- following reports that nearly 30 Reapers have been lost in combat against Iran (Breaking Defense, Jul 8 2026). The MMA is not described as a direct Reaper replacement but is envisioned for strike, reconnaissance, and electronic warfare missions. The solicitation calls for a payload of at least 2,800 pounds, an unrefueled combat range of at least 2,300 nautical miles, full-scale prototype flight testing within 21 months of award, and an initial operating capability targeted at FY2031. Responses are due Jul 23 2026. The programme uses an Other Transaction agreement to encourage non-traditional contractors.
◆ PRISM ANALYSIS
With 30 Reapers reportedly lost and an active combat requirement confirmed, the DIU notice represents one of the most urgent and concrete near-term drone procurement signals of 2026, favouring manufacturers with attritable ISR or strike UAS already at or near production readiness.
