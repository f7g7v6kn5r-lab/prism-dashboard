BREAKING North America CRITICAL · 97
Rocket Lab acquires Iridium for $8 billion
Rocket Lab announced on June 29, 2026 a definitive agreement to acquire Iridium Communications in a cash-and-stock transaction valued at approximately $8 billion in enterprise value, at $54 per Iridium share (half cash, half Rocket Lab stock), representing a 24.1% premium. The deal has been unanimously approved by both boards but is not yet closed; completion is expected in mid-2027, subject to Iridium stockholder approval and required regulatory approvals including FCC and ITAR review. The combined entity would hold launch, spacecraft manufacturing, and Iridium's 66-satellite LEO constellation, globally licensed L-band spectrum, and over 2.55 million subscribers spanning government, defense, aviation, maritime, and IoT customers.
◆ PRISM ANALYSIS
This deal structurally repositions Rocket Lab as a direct competitor to SpaceX across launch, constellation operations, and defense communications services, forcing a re-rating of every pure-play space-services name in the sector.
