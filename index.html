Starship Flight 13 aborts on pad at ignition
SpaceX scrubbed Starship Flight 13 on July 16, 2026 after an automated abort at T-0 when multiple Raptor 3 engines on Booster 20 failed to ignite; four engines did not light, exceeding the vehicles tolerance threshold, according to Space.com and CNN.
