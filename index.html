SpaceX launches Northrop MRV-1 satellite servicer
SpaceX successfully launched Northrop Grummans Mission Robotic Vehicle 1 (MRV-1) from Cape Canaveral on Tuesday, sending a two-armed space robot toward geostationary orbit on a DARPA-backed mission. BREAKING Middle East CRITICAL · 92
Trump approves nuclear deal allowing Saudi uranium enrichment
President Trump has approved a nuclear energy agreement with Saudi Arabia that, according to two AP sources, could allow uranium enrichment on Saudi soil under a US-backed framework, with formal public announcement expected as early as Wednesday July 22. The 30-year deal would involve US firms in developing the program and could allow construction of a uranium enrichment facility following a joint US-Saudi study, though enrichment could only proceed after that study confirms it is warranted and commercially viable, and sensitive technology would not be transferred to Saudi Arabia. The agreement, which does not include the IAEAs Additional Protocol for enhanced monitoring, is expected to be submitted to Congress for review and may face resistance from lawmakers concerned about proliferation risk (AP via multiple outlets, July 21 to 22 2026).
