SpaceX Launches Northrop MRV-1 Servicing Robot
A SpaceX Falcon 9 launched Northrop Grumman's Mission Robotic Vehicle 1, a dual-arm spacecraft designed to dock with aging geosynchronous communications satellites and attach propulsion modules to extend their operational lives by years. Iran War Cost Hits $37.5B, Pentagon Seeks $67.1B More
Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth confirmed before the Senate Appropriations Committee on Jul 21, 2026 that the US-Iran conflict has cost $37.5 billion to date and formally requested $67.1 billion in supplemental funding covering munitions, drones, operational costs, and border deployments. The request faces senatorial skepticism given the Pentagon retains approximately $75 billion from prior reconciliation funding. As of Jul 21, the US had conducted 10 consecutive nights of strikes on Iranian military and port infrastructure; the claim of an eleventh consecutive night is not yet confirmed by a live source as of Jul 22. Iranian forces have struck US installations in Kuwait and Jordan; Bahrain is not confirmed as a strike target in current sources. Pentagon figures put US service member injuries at nearly 100 since Jul 7, with 18 total US fatalities to date -- not injuries alone over two weeks as originally framed.
◆ PRISM ANALYSIS
A $67.1 billion supplemental request of this scale, if passed, would represent the single largest emergency defence injection since the post-9/11 period and would drive urgent procurement across missile defence, ISR, electronic warfare, and hardened communications — directly benefiting both primes and dual-use new-space companies with Gulf-region exposure.
