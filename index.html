SpaceX is targeting July 16, 2026, with a 90-minute window opening at 6:45 p.m. ET, for Starship Flight 13 from Starbase, Texas, carrying 20 Starlink V3 satellites -- not the first deployment on a suborbital trajectory, as the previous Flight 12 already debuted the V3 vehicle.
