On June 24, 2026, the Missile Defense Agency awarded Lockheed Martin a multi-year, sole-source, undefinitized contract with a ceiling of approximately 35.3 billion dollars to quadruple THAAD interceptor production from 96 to up to 400 per year through June 2032.
