Starship Flight 13 targets Raptor relight gate
SpaceX targeted Starship Flight 13 for launch no earlier than 6:45 p.m. EDT (2245 GMT) on Jul 16 from Starbase, Texas, in a 90-minute window. The mission flies a suborbital profile similar to Flight 12, with engineering objectives centred on correcting the booster separation sequence that caused Super Heavy to miss its splashdown zone on the previous flight -- not a Raptor Vacuum engine relight, which sources do not identify as a primary gate for this mission. The flight will also deploy 20 functioning Starlink V3 satellites, the first time Starship has carried operational commercial payload (SpaceNews, Jul 13; Space.com, Jul 15).
◆ PRISM ANALYSIS
A successful Raptor Vacuum relight would unlock Artemis crewed mission planning timelines and validate Starship as an operational deep-space transfer vehicle, directly defending NASAs multi-billion-dollar HLS contract and SpaceX equity near its IPO floor.

Starship Flight 13 deploys first Starlink V3 satellites
SpaceX targeted Starship Flight 13 for launch no earlier than Jul 16 to deploy 20 functioning Starlink V3 satellites -- not a first batch deployed on a prior suborbital flight. Senate blocks NDAA, $1.15T in weapons in limbo
Senate Democrats blocked the FY2027 NDAA on a 50-46 cloture vote on Jul 14, failing to reach the 60-vote threshold required to advance the bill -- the first such procedural failure in 66 consecutive years. The bill carries approximately 1.14-1.15 trillion dollars in defence authorisations. Senate Majority Leader John Thune switched his vote from yes to no as a procedural move preserving his right to refile the motion. Opposition was driven by both the Iran war authorisation dispute and disagreements over the defence spending topline, not solely Iran (Breaking Defense, Jul 15; Defense Daily, Jul 14).
◆ PRISM ANALYSIS
A prolonged NDAA failure creates immediate contract award uncertainty for LMT, RTX, NOC, LHX, and BA, as multiyear procurement authorities lapse — investors should treat this as a programme-funding tail risk even as operational demand signals remain strong.
