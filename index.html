Rocket Lab to Acquire Iridium in Landmark Deal
Rocket Lab and Iridium announced on June 29, 2026 that they have entered into a definitive agreement under which Rocket Lab will acquire all outstanding shares of Iridium at $54 per share in a cash and stock transaction, representing an enterprise value of approximately $8.0 billion. The deal, which is subject to Iridium shareholder approval and regulatory clearance and is expected to close by mid-2027, combines Rocket Labs vertically integrated launch and satellite manufacturing capability with Iridiums operational 66-satellite L-band constellation, creating one of the most capable end-to-end space services companies outside of SpaceX. Source: SEC Form 8-K filings and joint press release, June 29, 2026.
