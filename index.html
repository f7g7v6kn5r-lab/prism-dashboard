US-Iran fighting enters third day, Hormuz halts
Renewed combat between the United States and Iran has stretched into a third consecutive day after President Trump declared the ceasefire over on Jul 7, 2026, following Iranian attacks on three commercial vessels in the Strait of Hormuz (Britannica, Jul 9).
