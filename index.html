LMT wins $35B+ contract to quadruple THAAD output
On June 24, 2026, the Missile Defense Agency awarded Lockheed Martin a seven-year undefinitised contract action valued at up to $35.5 billion to quadruple production of the Terminal High Altitude Area Defense system, from 96 to 400 interceptors per year, per Defense Daily and Lockheed Martin official release. The award converts the January 29, 2026 framework agreement into a full procurement contract and is one of the first major multiyear contracts executed under the Department of War Acquisition Transformation Strategy. The award directly responds to munitions stockpile depletion from Operation Epic Fury and positions THAAD as a cornerstone of both US homeland and allied missile defence architecture going forward.
