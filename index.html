▲ BULL CASE  /  ▼ BEAR CASE ▾
▲ BULL CASE

▼ BEAR CASE
A launch failure or scrub past July 15 confirms SpaceX Transporter as the structurally irreplaceable rideshare backbone, reinforces Blue Origins funding pitch at a reported $130B valuation as the only credible US competitor narrative, and delays Indian sovereign launch independence by at minimum two to three years.
