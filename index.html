Starship Flight 13 aborts at ignition
SpaceX Starship Flight 13 suffered an abort during its Jul 16, 2026 launch attempt when four Raptor engines on Booster 20 failed to ignite, triggering an automatic abort before liftoff and prompting detanking (CNN, Jul 16, 2026). The mission profile remains a suborbital flight carrying 20 Starlink V3 satellites on a non-orbital trajectory; the satellites will reenter after deployment and the abort is a delay, not a mission cancellation.
