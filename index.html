US reimposing Hormuz blockade Iran threatens full closure
The US military early Wednesday reimposed a naval blockade scoped to Iranian ports and associated vessels -- not all traffic -- backed by strikes against dozens of Iranian military targets. CENTCOM confirmed a seven-hour overnight wave completed at 10 p.m. ET Tuesday, followed by a separate 90-minute daytime barrage on Jul 15 targeting coastal defense systems and cruise missile storage and launch sites on Greater Tunb Island. Trump warned of strikes on bridges and power plants next week unless Tehran returns to talks. The reimposed blockade is a second instance: a prior blockade ran from Apr 13 to Jun 18 before being lifted under a now-collapsed interim memorandum signed mid-June.
