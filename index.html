Starship Flight 13 second attempt underway
SpaceX is preparing its second attempt to launch Starship Flight 13, scheduled for Jul 23 2026 at 22:45 UTC from Orbital Launch Pad 2 at Starbase, Texas (Space.com, Jul 23 2026).
